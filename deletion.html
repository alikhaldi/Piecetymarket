Account Deletion Policy

At PiecetyMarket, you have full control over your personal data. If you wish to delete your account, here is how it works:

1. Request Deletion

You can request deletion of your account by contacting support at support@piecetymarket.com or through the account settings page.

2. What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account information and personal data will be permanently removed.
  • Your listings will be unpublished and no longer visible to other users.
  • Certain transactional data (such as completed sales) may be retained if required by law.

3. Timeline

We will process deletion requests within 30 days, unless a legal requirement prevents us from doing so immediately.

4. Reactivation

Once deleted, your account cannot be reactivated. You will need to create a new account if you return to PiecetyMarket.
